About Us

Who We Are

My Time is a pioneering technology company dedicated to transforming the way people manage their time, money, and daily lives. Founded with a vision to empower individuals and businesses through innovative mobile solutions, we are committed to delivering world-class apps that make life simpler, smarter, and more productive.

Our Mission

To create intuitive, secure, and powerful mobile applications that help users take control of their time, finances, and personal growth. We believe technology should serve people, not the other way around.

What We Do

  • Time Management: Our flagship app, My Time, helps users organize their schedules, set reminders, and achieve their goals with ease.
  • Financial Empowerment: Easy Money enables users to track expenses, manage budgets, and make smarter financial decisions.
  • Continuous Innovation: We are constantly researching and developing new features and apps to address real-world challenges and improve everyday life.
  • User-Centric Design: Every product is crafted with a focus on simplicity, security, and seamless user experience.

Our Achievements

  • Successfully launched multiple apps with thousands of downloads and positive user feedback.
  • Recognized for excellence in mobile app design and user experience.
  • Trusted by a growing community of users worldwide.

What We’re Working On

  • Expanding our app portfolio to cover health, productivity, and lifestyle domains.
  • Integrating AI and machine learning for smarter, more personalized user experiences.
  • Building partnerships with leading platforms and service providers.

Our Vision for the Future

We aim to become a global leader in mobile innovation, setting new standards for quality, reliability, and impact. Our goal is to empower millions to live better, more organized, and financially secure lives—one app at a time.
