Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download your apps?

+

You can download our apps directly from our Apps page. We offer direct APK downloads, and some of our apps are also available on the Amazon Appstore. We're working on making them available on Google Play Store soon.

How do I report a bug or issue?

+

You can report bugs or issues by sending an email to support@mytimes.app or by using the contact form on this page. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing.

Do you offer custom app development?

+

Yes, we offer custom app development services for businesses and individuals. Please contact us with your requirements, and we'll get back to you with a proposal.
